The monarch of the last standing civilization of the [[Sand Planet]]. Royal-looking lady. Strict, but effective, ruler. [[Sand Capital]], the capital city of the planet, is by far the most prosperous city still standing in the Solar System thanks to her reign. However, she imprisons suspected dissidents or even those who believe to have been in contact with the Radiance. She believe that the Radiance must be destroyed at all costs. You can side with her, or against her. Water type user.
