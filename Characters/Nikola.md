Daughter of the [[Zorya]] and next in line as empress of [[Djet]]. A free spirit, she's not too fond of her mother, or of much of anyone. She generally antagonizes you in your journey through Djet, but ultimately grows fond of you. While she in conflict with her mother, she also believes that getting rid of the Radiance is the only path forward for humanity. 

In the Law path[^1], her mother eventually loses her life in pursuit of the goal of getting rid of the Radiance, and she succeeds her, ruling over the known universe. In the Chaos path, she's the one that turns into an Ultra Beast: Kartana.

Grass type user: her signature pokemon is Maractus.

[^1]: See [[Story Paths]]

Name origin: from St Nicholas, associated with the deity Zorya in Slavic folklore
