Researcher on the [[Crepitus Space Station]]. She's studying the Radiance and its effects. Secretly, she's running experiments on biological creatures, that resulted in the creation of [[Deoxys]]. She takes samples of your DNA to study why you're immune to the Radiance, and injects it in a colony of Ditto cells.

Water-type user: her signature Pokemon is Starmie.

Name origin: Jengu, water spirit of healing in Cameroonian folklore
