- A long, long time after the events of Pokemon Emerald, Necrozma emerges from a black hole
- The will of the universe manifests Cosmon to defeat it
- The two fight, Cosmon loses but Necrozma is heavily wounded
- Necrozma starts heads towards the solar system
- Cosmon also gets there with the last of its strength but is destroyed by the effort. Its main body splits in two Cosmogs and falls on Earth. The rest of its pieces spreads in the solar system. One ends up in the past, in Mossdeep.
- In the past, the protagonists find the fragment which is part of what motivates them to become astronauts. They also occasionally have strange dreams due to the fragment.
- The space mission was only meant to go to the moon, but a mysterious portal opens and sends you to the future Earth.
- Right as you arrive, you meet two Cosmog, two seemingly completely powerless Pokemon. However, when you show the fragment, the two join together, and Cosmog grows stronger and starts being able to dispel the radiance.
- At the end of the game, once you're fully powered up and defeat necrozma and whatever, you use the full power of the legendary to send yourself from the past to the future.
